What is Metronix?

Metronix is a new Discord Bot that is full of multiple different features!
Here's a few features:

Easy access to the invite to add Metronix to your server.
Random variable picker to enhance the fun for members of your server.
Basic moderation commands like ban and kick to remove any trouble from your server.
